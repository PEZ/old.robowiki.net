  • That is what DynamicDistancing is about. Usually, if you're not firing, that should put you at long distance to your opponent. If your own movement is weaker at long distances, however (like FloodMini's), it may pick a more middle-range distance. Basically, the distance you would go to would be the point where (benefit-penalty)/(benefit+penalty) is the greatest number. If I surivive more than half the rounds, this number is likely positive. "benefit" in this case would be the enemy's expended energy from firing, "penalty" is the sum of energy lost by Fhqwhgads and energy gained by the enemy from hitting. -- Kawigi
