The Bot:

Great, I want to try it. Where can I download it?
http://designnj.de/robocode/Krabb.krabby2.Krabby2_1.9p.jar

How does it move?
It surfs perpendicular to the Enemy.
Tuned for "bad" aiming bots

How does it fire?
Using many segmented VirtualGuns(based on fired waves, no tick waves): CircularTargeting and GuessFactors
Tuned for "good" moving bots

How does it dodge bullets?
TrueSurfing

How does the melee strategy differ from one-on-one strategy?
No meele stategy

Can I use your code?
Yes, it's released under the RWPCL. (since V. 1.9h)

What other robot(s) is it based on?
On Krabby, but the name is the only remaining similarity.
