CircleBots

Robo Home | Changes | Preferences | AllPages
A circle bot simply circles as its movement. These bots are usually the second class of bot that beginners learn to target. It has the advantege of avoidng walls and being somewhat safe from linear aim routines. Many early sucessful NanoBots class robots use(d) this style of movement as it takes very little CodeSize to implement. See the sample SpinBot.
