What's special about it

It's a team of 1 GrubbmOgre (brains) and 4 GrubbmGrunts (muscles).

Great, I want to try it. Where can I download it?

mini.GrubbmGroup: http://www.robocoderepository.com/BotDetail.jsp?id=2483

How competitive is it?

Due to the number of contenders it will reach approx #10 in TeamRumble?.

How does it move?

Every teammember moves on its own, but when it collides, it bounces off.

How does it fire?

Every teammember fires on its own, but tries to hold fire when a teammate gets in the way.

How does it dodge bullets?

Not.

Does it have a Team strategy?

No, it is just a bunch of individuals and one of them has a megaphone.

How does it select a target to attack/avoid in Team?

Closest per individual or weakest when nearby.

What does it save between rounds and matches?

Nothing.

Where did you get the name?

I just love the sound of 'GR'.

Can I use your code?

It's in the jar.

What's next for your team?

- A real melee/team movement, probably anti-gravity.

Does it have any WhiteWhales?

SampleTeam? and myl.Troodon.

What other robot(s) is it based on?

GrubbmOne and GrubbmTwo for movement and targetting, myl.Troodon for team-communication.
