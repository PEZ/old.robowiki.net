How well can you hit Phoenix? Let's make it a challenge!

  • The Challenger must remain stationary all the time.
  • The Challenger must fire power 3 bullets when it can.
    • Which means no energy management whatsoever...
    • Challenger is only allowed to feed the fire() method(s) the constant 3. This results in one bullet per round fired with energy 1.
  • The Challenger meets Phoenix in battle over 500 rounds.
  • The Challenger's score is calculated like challenger_bullet_damage / rounds

Download the non-firing version of Phoenix here: http://davidalves.net/robocode/robots/davidalves.Phoenix_0.855TC.jar
