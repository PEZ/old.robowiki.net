My first pattern matching bot. Conceptually, it's based on MogBot, as it uses velocity and heading change. Its movement is based on MostlyHarmlessNano, which uses a timer.

Version History:

2004 04 28: version 0.12:

  • Simplified the movement, improved the radar, and adjusted the gun. The movement is flatter, but is more vulnerable to HeadOnTargeting. The pattern matcher weightings are less biased in favour of current data. Perhaps this version will be better, and not worse.

2004 04 24: version 0.11:

  • Tweaked the movement and the gun, but especially the movement.
  • Rating: 1638 (micro), 1622 (general)

2004 04 22: version 0.1:

  • Don't expect this version to be any good - my first releases are usually terrible.
  • Rating: 1660 (micro), 1626 (general)
