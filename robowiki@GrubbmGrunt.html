Description

GrubbmGrunt is a derivate of GrubbmTwo with the focus on team-capabilities for GrubbmGroup.

Great, I want to try it. Where can I download it?

mini.GrubbmGrunt: http://www.robocoderepository.com/BotDetail.jsp?id=2480

How competitive is it?

Anonymously just above 1600.

How does it move?

Oscillator movement with some wall-avoidance.

How does it fire?

Linear / circular targeting.

How does it dodge bullets?

Not.

How does the melee strategy differ from one-on-one strategy?

No melee strategy. Its team strategy however is remotely controlled by the teamleader.

How does it select a target to attack/avoid in melee?

First one it sees, until someone closer gets in the way.

What does it save between rounds and matches?

Nothing

Where did you get the name?

I just love the sound of 'GR'. A Grunt (WarCraft?) can hit you hard, but is not very intelligent.

Can I use your code?

It's in the jar.

What's next for your robot?

Optimizing the teamstrategy.

Does it have any WhiteWhales?

Only as a team.

What other robot(s) is it based on?

GrubbmTwo for movement, GrubbmOne for targetting, myl.Troodon for team-communication.
