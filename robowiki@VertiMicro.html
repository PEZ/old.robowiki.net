How does it move?

It drives up and down the battle field, trying to clone the enemy's Y coordinate.

How does it fire?

A segmented GuessFactorTargeting gun. Since it clones the enemy's Y coordinates, the segmentation is quite small and specialized.

How does it dodge bullets?

Depending on the enemy, this bot dodges its own bullets at times.
