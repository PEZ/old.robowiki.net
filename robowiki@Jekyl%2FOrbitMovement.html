Jekyl/OrbitMovement

Robo Home | Jekyl | Changes | Preferences | AllPages
This type of movment is characterised by Jekyl staying roughly perpendicular to it's target and at a predifined distance. The result is that Jekyl tends to circle (orbit) a stationary bot. In actual practice, movement by the target will affect the distance between the two bots and thus the movement of Jekyl. Also thrown are parameters to bend the angle slightly, stop randomly, speed up randomly, reverse randomly, or otherwise make (a beginners) attempt to break pattern matching.
