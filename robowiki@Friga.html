Friga

the Norse mother goddess and the wife of Odin (Woden). One of the most important goddesses of Germanic religion, she was queen of the heavens, a deity of love and the household.

Just a little testbot hacked together this weekend based on mini.Wodan with some additional stuff to handle WaveSurfer bots a bit better.

  • v0.00 is the baseline: mini.Wodan movement and the mini.Wodan GF-gun with 30% more GF-segments + faster learning
  • v0.01z uses a random linear targetting gun
