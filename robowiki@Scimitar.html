What's special about it?

Features a very fast melee patternmatcher. (shouldn't slow down your battles like other patternmatcher do) It's very inaccurate, though, which makes this gun rather crappy.

Great, I want to try it. Where can I download it?

https://www.robocoderepository.com/BotDetail.jsp?id=1810

How competitive is it?

Not as good as the top melee MiniBots, but it's not bad either (I think).

How does it move?

I stole the movement idea from Zeb's MajorMarauder.
There are a bunch of 'sensors' around Scimitar that checks for 'heat' from enemy bots and walls. Every time Scimitar has finished moving, it checks all 'sensors' and moves to the spot with lowest 'heat'.

How does it fire?

It keeps track of the lateral displacement of all bots within scan range and fills a lookup-table with these displacements. Using the history of displacements of the target bot as indices, finding the target's next move takes very little time. This patternmatcher is extremely inaccurate, though, and needs quite some rounds to fill the lookup-table. Actually, the samplebots are just about the only bots Scimitar gets a decent hit-rate against. I'll try to shrink Parakeet's gun and fit it into Scimitar sometime in the future... this gun is just not worth the amount of bytes spent.
