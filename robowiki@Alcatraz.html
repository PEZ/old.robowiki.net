Me:

I'm 18, in college, and living in Pennsylvania in the United States. I've been following Robocode for about 4 years.

Bots:

Competitive Megabot(s):

  • Cyanide - My first WaveSurfing bot and best overall bot; as of October '05 it was #7 in the RR@H.
  • Immortal - My first good bot, it currently features a semi broken WaveSurfing mechanism; best ranking: #13 in the RR@H.
  • Virgin? - This bot doesn't really have a point, it's neither very original nor very good. As of June '05 it was ranked in the 60s in the RR@H.
  • Calliope - The most downloaded bot on the Repository, though that's due to a bug. This was my first even relatively competitive bot.

Competitive Minibot(s):

  • Cyprus? - A minibot in the 80's of RR@H as of June '05. It has had various lukewarm reincarnations.

Competitive Nanobot(s):

  • Freddie - The top nanobot by a good margin as of October '05. It switches between two movement types, one that avoids HeadOnTargetting? and one that avoids LinearTargetting?.

Competitive Robot(s):

  • Krazy - This is my ExtendsRobot bot. It is the consistent runner up in the ExtendsRobotCompetition that I occasionally run.

Competitive NGBot(s):
