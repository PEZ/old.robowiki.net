Mjolnir,

Thor's hammer is said to have the following powers: "it would never break, it would never be lost because it would return to Thor's hand after being thrown and it could be made smaller so Thor could keep it in his shirt..." .

How does it move?

Close combat.

How does it fire?

It uses a simple GuessFactor gun.

How does it dodge bullets?

It doesn't.

How does the melee strategy differ from one-on-one strategy?

Not capable of a melee fight.

What does it save between rounds and matches?

All Guessfactor data on the opponent.

Where did you get the name?

Mjolnir, or Thor's hammer, seemed like an appropriate description of its behaviour..

Where can i get it?

http://www.robocoderepository.com/BotDetail.jsp?id=2220

Can I use your code?

Of course, the source is in the package. Not much new though...
