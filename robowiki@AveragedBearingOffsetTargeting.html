AveragedBearingOffsetTargeting

Robo Home | Changes | Preferences | AllPages
As simple as Targeting gets - beyond HeadOnTargeting. Yet, surprisingly effective.
  • Keep book on the average difference in bearing to the enemy from when you fire to when the bullet is supposed to have hit.
  • Add this averaged bearing diff to the absolute bearing of the enemy and fire.
It can be improved by:
  • Using a rolling average.
  • Segmenting the statistics
    • Maybe by distance
    • Maybe by delta bearing from last scan (like in MakoHT/Code)
    • Maybe by both
    • Maybe something else (suggestions anyone?)
