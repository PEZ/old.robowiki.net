USA Education System

  • Preschool: Ages 3-4 (optional, not free)
  • Kindergarten: Age 5
  • Elementary School (Grades 1-6): Ages 6-12
  • Junior High School (Grades 7-8): Ages 13-14
  • High School (Grades 9-12): Ages 15-18 (also known as Freshman, Sophomore, Junior, and Senior years)
  • College: 18+, typically 4 years, but depends on subject and degree ( e.g. Associate, Bachelor, Master, Doctor )

Some people skip grades and others are held back, and birthdays throw off ages, but that's a general range. Private schools often span multiple education groupings, but public schools are generally broken down into elementary, junior high, and high schools. -- Martin Alan Pedersen
