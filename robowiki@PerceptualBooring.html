PerceptualBooring

My only perceptional bot. The first version was not perceptual though and I removed it prom the perceptional scene when I discovered what a preceptual bot was all about.

What's special about it?

The first perceptual bot who used the difference in energy between him and his opponent to choose different fighting strategies. Attacked when he was loosing, withdrew when he was ahead. Also changed strategies depeing on how long he had been fighting. Tuned for surviving only.

How competitive is it?

I have no idea. try it out :)

How does it move?

Side to side movement with a very small randomized factor was the beginning strategy. A running around strategy replaced that if it was unsuccessful (was to counter mimicing bots)

How does it fire?

Randomized fire between max linear prediction * 0.7 and enemys current location. Yeah he would never hit a circling but at full speed all the time.

How does it dodge bullets?

It didnt, just moved.

How does the melee strategy differ from one-on-one strategy?

Currently it doesnt have a melee strategy. only 1vs1.

What does it save between rounds and matches?

Nothing. Zip! Nada!!!!

Where did you get the name?

Was supposed to be a perceptiual version of Booring but since I had not fully understood the meaning of a perceptual bot, it couldnt be done, but the name was kept.
