SoloDroid

Robo Home | Changes | Preferences | AllPages
SoloDroid is a droid that extends robot. It moves using a pattern because droids usually do not have a pattern matcher and fires .1's to find a target and then 1's until it has shot a certain number of bullets.

This is accessible at the following:
https://www.geocities.com/seakinsen/linkpage.htm
https://www.robocoderepository.com/BotDetail.jsp?id=2606

Questions and Comments are welcome.
