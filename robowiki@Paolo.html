Paulo - by PEZ

Paulo Roberto is a, now retired, Swedish middle weight boxing champion. He punched hard and was hard to hit.

Paolo the robot tries to honour his model using a GuessFactorTargeting gun and AdaptiveMovement.

Don't expect this bot to be updated. It was too frustrating cramming all the fatures of this bot into a MiniBot case. It weights in at 1499 bytes. That's exactly what real world boxer try to do to, weight in just below the weight class limit. =)
