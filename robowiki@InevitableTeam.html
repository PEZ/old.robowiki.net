What's special about it?

Its a twin duel bot based off RaikoMicro

Great, I want to try it. Where can I download it?

http://www.dijitari.com/void/robocode/twinduel_012/wiki.twin.InevitableTeam_0.1.jar

How competitive is it?

Its not bad, it beats a number of better Twin-Dueler's, but overall it loses more then it wins.

How does it move?

It uses RaikoMicro's random movement combined witha few things I pullked from raiko and my own bots.

How does it fire?

It uses a guessfactor gun, which is a risk on my part, I deemed it safe enough as most bots in the twinduel are just clones.

How does it dodge bullets?

It uses the MusashiTrick, via RaikoMirco? and Raiko.

Does it have a specific TwinDuel strategy?

Yes, the two bots attempt to team up on one bot and get it out of the way and then just pick off the remaining bot. Inevitable's will never hit each other with thier bullets as per lots of annoying code I put in to have then avoid that.

How does it select a target to attack/avoid?

InevitableAlpha? chooses a target which InevitableBeta? picks up on and fires against, unless InevitableAlpha? is dead, in which case InevitableBeta? picks the enemy to fire at.

What does it save between rounds and matches?

GF gun bins between rounds.

Where did you get the name?

Guess :P

Can I use your code?

It's in the .jar, released under the RWPCL.

What's next for your robot?

I was thinking of adding in Minimun Risk Movement and better team work.

Does it have any WhiteWhales?

Not really, anything I could consider one could be justa bunch of flukes.

What other robot(s) is it based on?

RaikoMicro
