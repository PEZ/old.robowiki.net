RoussGT and DrugeDC are what happen when you make hybrids of DrussGT and RougeDC. RoussGT has the gun of RougeDC and the movement of DrussGT. DrugeDC has the gun of DrussGT and the movement of RougeDC.

RoussGT 1.0a:

  • Based off of RougeDC Gamma4a and DrussGT 1.1.3
  • 2132.0 RR points (DrussGT 1.1.4 minus 30, RougeDC Gamma4a plus 58)
  • 623/0/8 PL (1 win better than DrussGT 1.1.4, 17 better than RougeDC Gamma4a)

DrugeDC 1.0:

  • Based off of RougeDC Gamma4a and DrussGT 1.1.3
  • 2096.69 RR points (DrussGT 1.1.4 minus 65, RougeDC Gamma4a plus 23)
  • 605/0/26 PL (17 wins worse than DrussGT 1.1.4, 1 worse than RougeDC Gamma4a)

RoussGT 1.1:

  • Based off of RougeDC Gamma2 and DrussGT 1.1.3
  • 2134.15 RR points (DrussGT 1.1.4 minus 27, RougeDC Gamma4a plus 60)
  • 629/0/4 PL (6 wins better than DrussGT 1.1.4, 21 wins better than RougeDC Gamma4a)

Note, that the comparisons are compared to the results at a particular point in time and are not necessarily stable, particularly the PL results.
