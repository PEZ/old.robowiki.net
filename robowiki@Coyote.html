What's special about it?

It is designed to fight on a 2 bot team for the TwinDuel competition.

Great, I want to try it. Where can I download it?

You can't at the moment.

How competitive is it?

I don't believe that it will be highly competitive at first, but I aim to change that.

How does it move?

My own interpretation of MinimumRiskMovement.

How does it fire?

It uses a highly compacted and simplified version of Engineer's Neural Net based gun.

How does it dodge bullets?

It slams into them usually.

How does the melee strategy differ from one-on-one strategy?

It doesn't

How does it select a target to attack/avoid in melee?

It's mostly based on distance, with a preference for the current target.

What does it save between rounds and matches?

The gun's Neural Nets are saved between rounds. Nothing is saved between matches.

Where did you get the name?

Coyotes are highly skilled predators that are known to hunt in pairs or small groups.

Can I use your code?

No.

What's next for your robot?

Being better.

Does it have any WhiteWhales?

It will soon.

What other robot(s) is it based on?

It's gun is based on the gun in Engineer.
