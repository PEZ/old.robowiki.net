What's special about it?

  • It was first started as a 1v1 extends robot similar to sample.MyFirstRobot, and then melee robot, it goes to corner first and then do what MyFirstRobot does. That behavior is not that good as turning takes time taking damage in the meanwhile. So I changed it not to change heading.

  • Corner oscillation, FiringAssistance, goTo
  • No wall collision damage of course.
  • Wider moving range than DuelistNanoMelee
  • Faster average velocity than DuelistNanoMelee due to wall collision causing instant stop.
