AdjustRadarForGunTurn

Robo Home | Changes | Preferences | AllPages
setAdjustRadarForGunTurn(true) will automatically turn the radar to compensate for gun turn. This means that if you turn your gun turret, your radar will stay pointed in the same direction. Note that setAdjustRadarForRobotTurn?(true) is not needed if you call setAdjustRadarForGunTurn(true) and setAdjustGunForRobotTurn(true)
