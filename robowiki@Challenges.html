Tired of trying to beat TheBestBot? Looking for way to measure your bot's performance? Try one of our Challenges!

Controlled Testing Environments

These challenges don't use standard robocode rules. For example, one bot may be prohibited from moving. They offer a great way to measure one aspect of your bot's performance at a time.

  • MovementChallenge: A measure of how good your bot's movement is, made up of:
    • CurveFlatteningChallenge: Try to avoid being hit by one of the GuessFactor guns around
    • AntiPatternMatcherChallenge: Try to avoid being hit by the champion of the PatternMatcherChallenge
  • WaveSurfingChallenge: Can you dodge *every* bullet these bots fire?
  • TargetingChallenge: Think you have the best aim around?
  • PatternMatcherChallenge: Try to beat this bot... without moving! Luckily it moves in a perfectly predicatable pattern, so if you can hit it with every single shot you just might win.

Standard Battles

These challenges use standard robocode rules. Try to get a higher score vs. the other bot than anyone else!
  • DevilFISHChallenge: Show off your close combat skills vs. DevilFISH
  • BarracudaChallenge: Barracuda has strong movement but a buggy gun... exploit the bug and dodge his bullets!
  • SandboxDT/Challenge: Try and beat one of the strongest bots of all time.
  • SharkChallenge: Got your WaveSurfing working? Great! But what good is surfing against a shark?

"extends Robot" Challenges

These challenges force you to extend the Robot class, rather than AdvancedRobot or TeamRobot.

  • SpinBotChallenge: Can you get a perfect score against SpinBot?
  • SampleBotMeleeChallenge: Get the highest possible score against all the sample bots in a 1000x1000 field
