PromptingStatement

A wiki technique to ask for information. Leaving a link to a new page without filling it in is one way to try make someone fill the page with content. Just filling a new page with the simplest of questions or comment is another. The latter has the benefit that the new page shows up on the Changes page. -- PEZ
