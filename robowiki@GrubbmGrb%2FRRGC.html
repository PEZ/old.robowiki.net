GrubbmGrbs entry in the RRGunChallenge. So its basically Raikos movement without the MusashiTrick (see RaikoNMT) plugged into GrubbmGrb. This is also a test platform for some tryouts with targeting that are not yet available in a public release of GrubbmGrb.
