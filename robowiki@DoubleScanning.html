I'm trying to figure out what would cause you to receive two ScannedRobotEvents? for the same robot in a tick? -- Goofy

I've never got two ScannedRobotEvents? for the same bot in one tick (I suppose you mean ticks?). -- Jonathan

I've seen that now and then. And I'm too very curious about why that happens. I think I have asked that elsewhere on the wiki and got an answer, but I forgot it! =) -- PEZ

If you skip a turn before handling the ScannedRobotEvent for that turn, you'll get 2 ScannedRobotEvents? on the next turn. You only miss lose events if you skip 2 or more turns in a row, if you miss one you'll just get extra events on the next turn. --David Alves

Thanks for the explanation, David -- Goofy
