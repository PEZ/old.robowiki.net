HOA

Robo Home | Changes | Preferences | AllPages
HeadOnAvoidance?, also known as HeadOnTargetingAvoidance, is the technique of avoiding to be hit by the simpliest kind of targeting: HeadOnTargeting. HOA became the most important mantra for WaveSurfing bots, since it can show how perfect is your WaveSurfing. PEZ was one of the first RoboCoders to notice how profitable can be HOA in the current RoboRumble rating system.

See also WaveSurfing/BarracudaChallenge.
