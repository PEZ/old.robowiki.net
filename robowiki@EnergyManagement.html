EnergyManagement

Keeping your energy is a good way to improve survival (or avoid getting disabled). Many bots use different tricks to achieve this:
  • Fire low power bullets at long distances.
  • Never fire bullets with a power higher than the necesary to kill the enemy.
  • Reduce firepower (or not fire at all) when the bot energy is low.
