What's special about it?

It has a homegrown battlefield tracking system, where it keeps track of enemy energy levels, positions, headings, etc. Almost everything in it is homegrown.

How competitive is it?

Its not bad. Can't beat any megabots, but it can take on all 11 sample bots in a melee ;-)

Strike-series Random Motion System

How does it dodge bullets?

It curves around bullets. It knows when a bullet is coming by change in enemy energy level.

It selects based on proximity.
