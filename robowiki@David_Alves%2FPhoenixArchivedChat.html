Here are some tips to help you improve your WaveSurfingChallenge scores:

  • Run away at the beginning of the round: All bots start at gun heat 30, so you have a fair amount of time before you need to worry about moving perpendicular to the enemy.
  • Surf multiple waves: This is especially important in avoiding the one 1.0 power bullet that the WaveSurfingChallenge bots fire at the end of the round.
  • Don't segment your surfing too much: Figure out what really matters, and only segment on that.
  • Do NOT get trapped in corners: 1000 rounds is plenty of time to get that unlucky round where your initial positions have the challenge bot packing you tight into a corner. If your surfing code doesn't handle this properly, you're going to eat a lot of bullets.
