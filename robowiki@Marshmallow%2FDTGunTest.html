Paul Evans had the kindness to run some tests of Marshmallow movement that I asked of him. Below are the results. They show how well SandboxDT can hit three robots at various ranges; DT 1.81, DT 1.71 and Marshmallow 1.5.4. DT has about equally hard to hit DT 1.71 as it has with M 1.5.4. Unfortunately the new DT outperforms them both by a comfortable margin...
