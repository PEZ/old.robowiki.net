Krazy, a Robot.

1.5 beats all the other Robots I know of (most of the time). I assume. Its scores against Kawigi's Girl range from 35-65 to 65-35 so the next few ExtendsRobotCompetitions? should be interesting.

Author

Alcatraz

What's special about it?

It's only a Robot!

How competitive is it?

Not very. In fact, it sucks. But that's only cause it's fighting ADVANCED bots.

How does it move?

Avoids the walls like Raiko does, except in Robot version. Switches directions occasionally.

How does it fire?

Statistical targetting based off of FloodMini.

How does it dodge bullets?

Randomly.

How does the melee strategy differ from one-on-one strategy?

It doesn't.

How does it select a target to attack/avoid in melee?

Whoever is closest.

What does it save between rounds and matches?

Nothing.

Where did you get the name?

This was the name of my first bot way back two years ago, it was worse than this one, I think.

Can I use your code?

Yes.

What's next for your robot?

Beating a few advanced bots.

Does it have any WhiteWhales?

Advanced bots.

What other robot(s) is it based on?

None.
