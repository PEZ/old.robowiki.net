Greywhind has completed about 5 different bots, each improving on the last. He dreams of breaking the top 100 in 1v1 RoboRumble@Home (which he did with StrengthBee?, but that's cheating because it's not his gun). Unlike many Robocoders, he doesn't care about code size at the moment. He's more interested in finding new methods and actually making a competitive robot. So far, he has used LaserTargeting, GuessFactorTargeting, and VirtualGuns. For movement, he likes WaveSurfing the best.

My Robots:

  • LineofFire: Uses movement based on avoiding areas where it has been hit (terrible) and GuessFactorTargeting segmented on distance (also terribly done).
  • Dexterity?: An implementation of Bullet Dodging without EnemyWaves. It assumes you fire head on and dodges. Surprisingly successful.
  • Dexterity06: Dexterity with a multiple choice gun.
  • Charisma?: Dexterity06 with LineofFire's gun. Surprisingly, worse.
  • Wisdom: Dexterity06 with a LaserTargeting gun.
  • Constitution: Dexterity06 with a virtual gun array.
  • Strength: Constitution's guns (for now), new WaveSurfing movement.
Also, a small group of Haikus that aren't online at the moment.
