Tad was one of a few people that started corresponding with me via email after I released Duelist. After a few emails I stopped getting questions from him, so I assumed he'd lost interest in Robocode. Silly me! Turns out he was busy writing Dalek, a superb 1-v-1 bot. I'm posting the original emails here (TadsEmails) because I think they might be useful for other people to read - we discussed a lot of good theoretical topics. --David Alves
