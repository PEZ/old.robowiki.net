How competitive is it?

I'm expecting #1. I tested with the best bots in the nano melee rumble, and I consistently took 1st place. Given that they comprise almost 1/2 the field, my hopes are up. But we'll see.

How does it move?

Ah, now this is inspired by one of the first bots I ever downloaded, DuelistNanoMelee. It oscillates back & forth like MyFirstRobot, but it does so in the corner. Unlike the duelist, though, MeleeSeed varies the length of its movement based on distance to the enemy.

How does it fire?

HeadOnTargeting.

How does it dodge bullets?

The theory is that he should move as far as possible each direction to avoid HeadOnTargeting, but stop just short of being susceptible to LinearTargeting / CircularTargeting (the same, since he mostly moves in straight lines).

How does the melee strategy differ from one-on-one strategy?

Come on, this is a nano for goodness sake! Melee only.

How does it select a target to attack/avoid in melee?

The closest bot.

What does it save between rounds and matches?

Nothin.
