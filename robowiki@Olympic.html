Hi you guys.As this year there will be the Olympic Games in Athen I thought it would be a nice thing to run a similar challenge with robocode.Here is what it will look like:
  • all robocoders from one nation are together a team.
  • every nation-team votes for some kind of leader that is responsible for the final robots that will take part in the challange.
  • the team writes/discusses its robots together.
  • all robots that want to participate have to be open source.
  • field events are: 1v1, melee maybe a quick 1v1 ( with only 1 round ) , others may follow

please tell me what you think about it.

--deathcon
