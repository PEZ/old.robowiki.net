DookiSerpent

A bot by Kev and Voidious, using WaveSerpent's movement and Dookious's gun.

Version History:

  • 1.22, rated 2099, ranked 3 (7 PL).
    • -18 points from Dookious, 7 more premier league losses than it.
    • +31 points from WaveSerpent, 4 more premier league wins than it.
    • Uses the movement of WaveSerpent 1.22
    • Uses the gun of Dookious 1.472

Download at: http://www.cs.utexas.edu/users/pclark/wiki.DookiSerpent_1.22.jar
