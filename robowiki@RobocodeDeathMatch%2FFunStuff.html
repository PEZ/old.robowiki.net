Kill Message

%Killer will be replaced by the killer bot name, and %Victim by the name of the victim (Note case match). You can enter html code also. 
%Victim bit %Killer's bullet
%Killer 0wn3d %Victim
%Victim was %Killer's clay pigeon
%Victim's chassis was punctured by %Killer's bullet
%Killer smacked down %Victim
%Victim ate a bullet and %Killer's sandwich
%Killer made <font color = "yellow">Swiss cheese</font> of %Victim's chassis
%Killer put %Victim into therapy
%Victim was sacrificed to %Killer's roboGod
%Killer melted %Victim's circitry

Suicide Message

%Robot will be replaced by real robot name. you can also enter html code. 
%Robot sacrifice itself to his roboGod
%Robot had a melt down
"%Victim ate a bullet and %Killer's sandwich" should probably be "%Victim ate %Killer's bullet sandwich." It has reference to the slang term of eating a knuckle sandwich. -- Kawigi
