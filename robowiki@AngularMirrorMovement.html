AngularMirrorMovement

Robo Home | Changes | Preferences | AllPages
A type of MirrorMovement where you copy just the angle of your opponent.

Sample code: 

setTurnRightRadians(Utils.normalRelativeAngle(e.getHeadingRadians() - getHeadingRadians()));

stelo.MirrorNano 1.2 uses this method.
