Oh, found a nice after Christmas Joke if anyone would like. If santa exists: 1) There are approximately 2 billion kids in the world (people under 18 years) on the earth. But Santa doesn’t have to visit Muslims, Hindu, Jewish or Buddish people which will reduce the number to 15 % or 378 millions. With a world average of 3,5 kids per house it will be 108 million homes to visit as we assume there’s at least one nice child in each house.

2) Santa has, because of the earth’s rotation and different time zones, around 31 hours to do his job, as we assume he travels from east to west which would be the most logical thing to do. That would mean that his got to visit 967,7 houses per second. So for each Christian house with a nice kid Santa has 1/1000 of a second to: Park the sledge, jump out, get down through the chimney, fill the socks, put the rest under the tree, eat the candy that’s been put out for him, get back up to the roof, jump back into the sledge and continue to the next house. As we assume that the homes are evenly spread around the world (which we know they are not, but we say so because it’s easier to calculate with) there will be 1 kilometre between each house in average. That will give us a journey of 100 million kilometres, as we don’t take into account toilet breaks and pauses as a part of our calculations. Therefore the sledge should fly approximately 1000 km per sec or 3000 times faster than the speed of sound. For compare, the fastest manmade machine, the space vessel the Ulysses, travels with around 50 km per sec. Just to inform, a normal reindeer can as a maximum run around 30 km/h.

3) The payload is interesting too. As we assume that each kid gets a toy-kit of medium size (1 kilo), therefore the sledge should be able to carry around 500.000 tons, without Santa himself. On land a reindeer can as a maximum pull 200 kilo. Even if we assume that Santa’s special flying reindeers can pull 10 times as much, the job can’t be done by 8-9 reindeers - Santa would need 360.000 reindeers. This will, along with the presents bring the total weight up to 600.000 tons.

4) 600.000 tons, that travels with 1000 km/sec generates a massive amount of air resistance. This will heat up the reindeers as a meteor, entering the earth’s atmosphere. The two leading reindeers will absorb 14.300.000.000 billion joules of energy pr. sec, or 14.300.000.000 watts. They would simply flame up, and the reindeers behind would be victims of a supersonic boom and flame up too. The whole reindeer pack would be burned up in 4,26 thousands of a second, that is when Santa reaches house number 5. But that all doesn’t matter, because when Santa’s sledge accelerates from 0 to 1000 km/sec in such a short time, all in the sledge would be put under a force compared to 17.500 times of gravity. A Santa ways around 150 kg (based on known pictures) would be nailed to the backend of the sledge with a force of 3 million kilos, which would immediately crush every bone in his body and turn him into a pile of splat...

5) Conclusion is therefore: If Santa has ever existed, his dead now...
