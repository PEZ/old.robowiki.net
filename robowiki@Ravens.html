two.Ravens

Huginn and Muninn sit on Wodan's shoulders in this illustration from an 18th century Icelandic manuscript.

Huginn and Muninn are a pair of ravens associated with the Norse god Wodan (Odin). Hugin and Munin travel the world bearing news and information to Wodan. Hugin is "thought" and Munin is "memory". They are sent out at dawn to gather information and return in the evening. They perch on the god's shoulders and whisper the news into his ears..

How does it move?

Huginn and Muninn v0.1 use a variation of MinimumRiskMovement. Huginn and Muninn v0.2 use a variation of MinimumRiskMovement combined with orbital movement.

How does it fire?

Huginn and Muninn use mini.Freya's version of a GF-gun.

How does it dodge bullets?

They don't.

How does the melee strategy differ from one-on-one strategy?

The melee strategy currently does not differ from one-on-one strategy.

What does it save between rounds and matches?

Only Guessfactor data on the opponent. No data saveing between matches.

Where did you get the name?

All my bots are named after the Norse gods. The names are choosen such as to describe there properties as close as possible. See the [Wikipedia] for a complete description of Wodan's ravens.

What is it based on?
