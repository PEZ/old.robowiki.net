What's special about it

It's a lubricant to make others perform better in the team GrubbmGroup.

Great, I want to try it. Where can I download it?

mini.GrubbmOgre: http://www.robocoderepository.com/BotDetail.jsp?id=2484

mini.GrubbmGroup: http://www.robocoderepository.com/BotDetail.jsp?id=2483

How competitive is it?

Not, it is happy when it can cooperate with others.

How does it move?

Oscillator movement with some wall-smoothing.

How does it fire?

HOT-gun.

How does it dodge bullets?

Not.

How does the melee strategy differ from one-on-one strategy?

Not. Its team strategy however is to inform all others about the locations of the emenies.

How does it select a target to attack/avoid in melee?

Closest with a small preference for current and weak targets.

What does it save between rounds and matches?

Nothing

Where did you get the name?

I just love the sound of 'GR'. An Ogre cooperates with Grunts.

Can I use your code?

It's in the jar.

What's next for your robot?

Optimizing the teamstrategy (Done in v0.4). A real 'melee-radar' (Done in v0.3).

Does it have any WhiteWhales?

Only as a team.

What other robot(s) is it based on?

GrubbmOne and GrubbmTwo for framework, myl.Troodon for team-communication.
