Targeting

Quick (not to say unuseful) Targeting techniques that utilize information gained from only a few scans of information

    • RandomTargeting
  • More involved
    • Mean Targeting

Statistic Based Targeting techniques that utilize a build up of statistics (usually firing angle)

    • BestPSpace
    • LaserTargeting
    • AngularTargeting
    • AngularTargeting/Factored
    • AveragedBearingOffsetTargeting

Log Based Targeting techniques that utilize the examination of detailed enemy records

  • LogTargeting
    • PatternMatching
    • HistoricalVelocityRecal
    • DisplacementTargeting
    • AreaTargeting (aka MinimumRiskTargeting)

Heuristics Based Targeting techniques based on heuristical algorithms

    • NeuralTargeting
    • GeneticTargeting
    • FuzzyLogicTargeting

Hybrid methods Targeting techniques that try to combine the best of Log and Statistical targeting

  • SymbolicDynamicSegmentation
  • ZoomTargeting
  • SplitTargeting
  • EscapeEnvelope
  • GuessFactor2D
  • SmartFactorTargeting
  • AntiSurferTargeting
