GirlPower

The driving force behind the naming of several bots, notably Furia_Ceca, ScruchiPu, LauLectrik and NanoLauLectrik.

I asked my girlfriend to give me a name for a bot and got something along the lines of ScruchiPu. I decided to call it Immortal instead. But at least she still puts up with me and my robocoding. -- Alcatraz

I just discovered that Ares (winner of the Novice division of the IBM Robocode Rumble) was named by GirlPower. From the IBM website:

"Because another 'bot was already named Vendetta, I decided to choose another name. My girlfriend chose the name of the Greek god of war, and Ares was born." -- Manfred Schuster, Germany

-- Kawigi
