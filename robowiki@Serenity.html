Serenity

Intro

Serenity is a non-agressive bot. It doesn't harm anybody on purpose, but it just happens to bump into someone sometimes. You can use it for target-practice, because it continuously tells you where it is at what tick. I want to use it to test evasive algorithms. It can be found here.

Movement

Serenity implements simple evasive manouvres, so it's not as easy to hit as SittingDuck. Just try it!

Firing

Serenity is a peaceful bot! What are you thinking! ;-)
