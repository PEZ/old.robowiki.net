RoboRumble@home

The ultimate collaborative effort to have a live, up-to-date ranking of bots. It uses the power of available robocoder's computers to distribute the effort of running battles and building the rankings.

  • To enter the competition, go to /EnterTheCompetition.
  • To join RR@H and help running the matches, go to /StartingWithRoboRumble.
  • To see the current rankings, go to /CurrentRankings.
  • To read the rules for bots entering the competition, go to /Rules
  • If you are experiencing some problem, go to /ReportedProblems to see the solution or adding yours.
  • If you want to collaborate in the further development of RR@H, go to /Development.
  • Just for fun, go to /Chat or /RankingChat.
