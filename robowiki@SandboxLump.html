Entered in IBM's robocode rumble - not a bad competitor but relied too heavily on learning and saving enemy data between rounds (rumble required quick learning from scratch over 10 rounds for the final). At the time only one or two bots could beat it in the long run (blotbot was one, but did not qualify because of its melee performance) - SandboxDT soon put a stop to this.

-- Paul Evans
